RowdyHacks

RowdyHacks is ACM UTSA's annual hackathon held in April.

Every year, a committee of ACM members get together to help plan and organize the biggest hackathon in San Antonio, TX accommodating hundreds of students, sponsors, volunteers, and mentors.

Interested in sponsorship or being a RowdyHacks organizer? Reach out to us at team@rowdyhacks.org to see how you can get involved.
