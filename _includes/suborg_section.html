Our Sub Organizations

  • ACM-W

    Minority centric chapter focusing on creating an academic and professional community

  • ICPC

    Competitive programming for students looking to expand their coding skills

  • Rowdy Creators

    ACM's multi-discipline incubator specializing in helping students learn and contribute to long-term technology projects

  • Coding in Color

    We strive to create a safe space for black people in the technology world to promote representation in our community.

  • RowdyHacks

    ACM UTSA's 24-hour hackathon competition with hundreds of students, sponsors, and prizes

  • Code Quantum

    ACM UTSA's first annual hackathon for underrepresented genders
